DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

Information ScIENCE AND ENGINEERING

VISION

"To mould information technologists empowered by values and technologies for the welfare of global society."

Mission

The mission of Department of Information Science and Engineering, Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Bidar are to:
M1: Equip the students with knowledge base of latest technologies to develop novel applications
M2: Inculcate professional ethics in students.
M3: Motivate and train students to become probable entrepreneurs.

ABOUT Department

The department of ISE which came in to being in the year 2000 with the intent of churning out engineering graduates equipp with the right skill set to cater to the booming software industry around. This Department, with an annual intake of 60, boasts of well qualified faculty, well equipped laboratories and smart class rooms, aims to offer it's students the best learning experience. Moreover, guest lectures from the renowned industrial experts, workshops, and assistance offered for internships & placements by the enterprising faculty make it the most preferred destination in this region. Our department alumni placed in some of the top companies like Infosys, TCS, Accenture, cognizant, Dell, Intel and many others, stand testimony to it.
